Three ways to practice

Pick what fits the day: solo speaking, a back-and-forth with Buddy, or a playful agree/disagree moment.

🎙️

My Voice

Random kid-friendly prompts, record your answer, listen back, and hear Buddy repeats—your words echoed clearly so you can hear how you sound.

💬

Let’s Chat

Talk to Buddy with the big mic. Short, encouraging replies keep the conversation moving—great for warming up before school or winding down after.

⚡

Quick Debate

Silly topics, pick a side (agree or disagree), say why—and Buddy chimes in with a kind, playful response. Low stakes, lots of laughs.

Why we built it this way

Communication practice works best when it feels safe, short, and a little fun.

  • Large tap targets and readable type—easy to use without squinting.
  • Short turns so energy stays high and attention doesn’t drift.
  • Positive framing: celebrate trying, not “perfect” speech.
  • Topics stay light—feelings, favorites, silly hypotheticals.

For grown-ups

myMic is a practice tool for speaking and conversation—not therapy and not a replacement for professional care. We keep chats kid-appropriate; you control device settings and any optional AI features (such as API keys) on your family’s devices.

Review screen time and privacy settings together. If something in the app worries you, talk with your child and adjust or remove access.
